# Social (Instagram) — Vena Digital

Templates for the three Instagram post families specified in the brand kit, plus a story template.

## Components
- `PostEducativo` — fondo claro, titular grande, palabra clave en coral, mascota pequeña, CTA "GUARDA ESTE POST"
- `PostOpinion` — fondo azul noche, frase opinada fuerte, autoría con foto Laura
- `PostLanzamiento` — fondo claro, mascota grande con chip "NUEVO", CTA "Ver academia"
- `Story` — 9:16 fondo coral, lockup invertido, "Desliza arriba"

Posts are designed at 1080×1350; rendered at 50% (540×675) in the gallery for layout reasons.
